Frequently Asked Questions

Registration

You can register online through our website's registration page. Early bird registration is available until March 31, 2025, at $35. Regular registration is $45 from April 1 to May 12, 2025.

Registration includes:

  • Race entry
  • Official race t-shirt
  • Timing chip
  • Finisher's medal
  • Post-race refreshments
  • Free race photos

Yes, registrations can be transferred to another runner until May 1, 2025, for a $10 processing fee. Contact us at teams@vaisakhi5kny.com to initiate a transfer.

Race Day

We recommend arriving at least 45 minutes before the 7:00 AM start time. This allows time for parking, bib pickup (if needed), gear check, and warm-up.

Parking is available at several garages near Central Park. We recommend using public transportation when possible. A parking map will be provided in your pre-race email.

Essential items include:

  • Race bib and safety pins
  • Timing chip
  • Running shoes and comfortable clothing
  • Weather-appropriate gear
  • Photo ID
  • Clear bag for gear check

Course

Yes, the Vaisakhi 5K course is USATF certified and accurately measured to 5 kilometers (3.1 miles).

Yes, there are three aid stations:

  • Mile 1: Water and sports drinks
  • Mile 2: Water and energy gels
  • Finish line: Water, sports drinks, and snacks

The course will remain open for 1 hour and 30 minutes, allowing for a 29-minute per mile pace. Participants must maintain this minimum pace to complete the race.

General

Vaisakhi is a significant cultural and religious festival celebrating the harvest season and the founding of the Khalsa (the community of initiated Sikhs) in 1699. Our race celebrates this tradition while promoting fitness and community unity.

We welcome volunteers! Please email volunteer@vaisakhi5kny.com with your interest. Volunteers receive a special t-shirt, refreshments, and our heartfelt appreciation.

Yes, a portion of each registration fee goes to local charities supporting education and food security initiatives. In 2024, we raised over $45,000 for our community partners.
